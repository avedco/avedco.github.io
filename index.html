How to Play

Welcome to Hangman! Here's how to play:

  • Guess the word by selecting letters.
  • You have 6 lives. For each incorrect guess, a part of the hangman is drawn.
  • Guess all the letters before the hangman is fully drawn to win.
  • Use hints if you need some help
  • Have fun!
